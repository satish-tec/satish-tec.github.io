Most recent work

MedPredict - Prediction of Disease Outbreaks

MedPredict is a predictive healthcare tool designed to analyze patient data and assess the risk of developing heart disease or diabetes. The core goal was to develop a lightweight yet robust AI model that can support early detection and proactive health monitoring.

Deepfake Voice Detection

ML-based system to detect deepfake voices using the FAKE or REAL (FOR) dataset. The baseline SVM model achieved an accuracy of 93.75 %, demonstrating strong generalization performance, while XGBoost outperformed all models with 96.25 % accuracy and high precision/recall balance. The Proposed Hybrid Model (CNN-LSTM), though slightly lower in overall accuracy 79.75 %, captures complex temporal and spectral features, which is critical for future scalability and real-time adaptability.
